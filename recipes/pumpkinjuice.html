Pumpkin Juice Recipe | 10 min | Serves 4

A magically refreshing and flavorful pumpkin juice to go along with pasties and chocolate frogs.

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 tbsp pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 cups apple juice

Instructions:

  1. Add the pumpkin puree, pumpkin pie spice, vanilla extract, and brown sugar to a mixing bowl.
  2. Stir in the apple juice and mix well until there are no lumps. Hand whisk makes it easy. Refrigerate the juice and enjoy the drink!
